How It Works

Your total score is calculated by adding your placement points to your kill points. For example, if you finish 3rd (10 points) and get 5 kills (5 points), your total score would be 15 points.

The higher your total score, the higher you'll rank on the leaderboard. Consistency is key - aim for high placements while maximizing your kills!
